Talks 2016

Call for proposals is open!

We are delighted to announce the call for proposals for our conference is open! Please submit your talk proposals, with the following guidelines.

  • Keynote: A keynote is a 30-60 minute talk on a broad topic that should be of interest to almost all of the attendees.
  • Talks have a 30 to 40 minutes duration plus 10 minutes for Q&A.
  • Tutorials/workshop are one and a half hours long and have a smaller venue with space for ten to twenty attendees.
  • We plan to make the entry fee voluntary for speakers, but this will be confirmed once we know more about our definitive budget.
  • You can edit your proposal while the CfP remains open.
  • You may submit several proposals.
  • Please use the following form to submit your proposal: http://bit.ly/2bWrzIa

The deadline for sending proposals has been extended to October 30th 2016. The organizing committee will review all proposals and will post a definitive schedule latest November 10th. 2016.
