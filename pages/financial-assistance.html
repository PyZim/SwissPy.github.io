Financial Assistance

Our aim is to make PyCon Zimbabwe accessible to everyone, irrespective of their financial situation. For this reason, we are providing multiple tiers of ticket prices, Student, Individual and Corporate. This is based on the principle that those who can afford to pay for tickets subsidize those who cannot. This structure enables us to include major diversity and outreach programmes such as DjangoGirls, reward and encourage active participation in the PyCon Zimbabwe event organization, and fund PyCon Zimbabwe’s Financial Aid programme.

PyCon Zimbabwe’s financial aid programme aims to provide funds to help people attend PyCon Zimbabwe.The process is designed to benefit people that we believe make PyCon Zimbabwe better, particularly speakers and workshop tutors, as well as notable open source contributors. Since we are running on a very limited budget, Pycon Zimbabwe will be providing Financial assistance to Students and attendees of Django Girls Harare only. We encourage all students or recent graduates who are applying for financial assistance to submit talk proposals so as to enhance their chances.

To submit a financial aid application, sign up or log in to your account and proceed to the Financial Aid section of your account dashboard.
